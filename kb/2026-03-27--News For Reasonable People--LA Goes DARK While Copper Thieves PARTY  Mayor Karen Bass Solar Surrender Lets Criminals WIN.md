## Summary

This episode criticizes Los Angeles Mayor Karen Bass's announcement of a $125 million initiative to install 60,000 solar-powered streetlights to combat copper wire theft. The host argues this approach avoids addressing the root problem of crime enforcement and represents a 'surrender' to criminals.
